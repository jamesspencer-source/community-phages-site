The people teaching the program.

Community Phages is taught by faculty sponsors, program leaders, lead instructors, graduate students, postdocs, staff, and alumni. The page is organized by teaching role so students and prospective instructors can see how the program works.

Program timeline Resources

A teaching team, not just a directory.

Faculty set scientific context, program leads keep the summer running, and mentors help students move from bench work to genome analysis and posters.

Teaching map How the team supports the program
01

Scientific direction

Faculty sponsors connect student discoveries to phage biology, bacterial evolution, immunity, and research context.

02

Program leadership

Program leads organize the lab environment, field-to-genome sequence, materials, safety, and daily teaching plan.

03

Mentor support

Instructors help students troubleshoot bench work, analyze genomes, build figures, and prepare final posters.
